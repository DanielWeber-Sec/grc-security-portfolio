# Regulatory Response

## Objective

Ensure compliance with legal and regulatory requirements during a security incident.

---

## Key Requirement (GDPR)

In case of a personal data breach:

- Notification to authorities within **72 hours**  
- Communication to affected individuals if high risk exists  

---

## Decision Criteria

### Is personal data affected?

- Yes → regulatory process triggered  
- No → internal handling only  

---

### Risk Assessment

- Type of data (sensitive vs. non-sensitive)  
- Volume of data  
- Potential impact on individuals  

---

## Responsibilities

| Task | Owner |
|------|------|
| Impact assessment | Security + Legal |
| Authority notification | Legal / Compliance |
| Customer communication | Management |
| Documentation | Incident Commander |

---

## Timeline

1. Incident detected  
2. Data breach assessment  
3. Legal evaluation  
4. Authority notification (≤ 72h)  
5. Customer notification (if required)  

---

## Risks of Non-Compliance

- Financial penalties  
- Legal consequences  
- Reputational damage  

---

## Key Principle

Regulatory response must be:

- Fast  
- Accurate  
- Documented  

---

## Key Takeaway

Incident response is not only technical.

It is also:

- Legal  
- Organizational  
- Reputational
