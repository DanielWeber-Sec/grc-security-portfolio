# Management Decision Log

## Incident: Ransomware Attack

---

## Decision 1

**Topic:** System shutdown  
**Options:**
- Keep systems running  
- Shutdown affected systems  

**Decision:** Shutdown systems  

**Rationale:** Limit further damage  

---

## Decision 2

**Topic:** Ransom payment  
**Options:**
- Pay ransom  
- Do not pay  

**Decision:** Do not pay  

**Rationale:** Legal risk + no guarantee  

---

## Decision 3

**Topic:** Communication  
**Options:**
- Internal only  
- Inform customers  

**Decision:** Inform stakeholders  

**Rationale:** Transparency + compliance  

---

## Key Insight

Management decisions directly influence:

- Financial impact  
- Legal exposure  
- Reputation
