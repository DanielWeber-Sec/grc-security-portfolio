# Containment

## Objective

Stop the spread of the attack and limit damage.

---

## Actions

- Disconnect affected systems from network  
- Disable compromised accounts  
- Block malicious IPs  
- Isolate infected environments  

---

## Challenges

- Lack of visibility  
- Unknown attack scope  
- Missing asset inventory  

---

## Result

- Attack spread stopped  
- Further damage prevented
