# ISO 27001 ↔ NIS2 Mapping

## Objective

This document demonstrates how ISO/IEC 27001 controls can support
NIS2 compliance requirements within modern organizations.

The goal is not a one-to-one legal interpretation,
but a governance-oriented alignment approach.

---

## Why This Matters

Organizations rarely implement a single framework.

In reality, companies must align:

- ISO/IEC 27001
- NIS2
- GDPR
- DORA
- BSI Grundschutz
- internal governance requirements

A structured mapping approach helps reduce duplication,
improve audit readiness,
and harmonize security governance.

---

## Governance Perspective

ISO 27001 provides the management system foundation.

NIS2 introduces regulatory obligations focused on:

- risk management
- incident reporting
- supply chain security
- business continuity
- management accountability

Together, they form a strong governance baseline.
