# Quarterly Management Review

> Governance-oriented quarterly management review focused on executive oversight, operational resilience, risk visibility, and continual ISMS improvement.

---

# Objective

This document demonstrates how organizations can structure periodic management review activities to support governance visibility, executive accountability, operational resilience, and risk-informed decision-making.

The review process helps ensure that ISMS governance remains aligned with:

- business objectives  
- operational realities  
- regulatory requirements  
- resilience expectations  
- enterprise risk exposure  

---

# Governance Perspective

Management reviews are not purely operational meetings.

They represent a formal governance mechanism used to evaluate:

- ISMS effectiveness  
- governance maturity  
- operational resilience  
- audit readiness  
- supplier risks  
- incident trends  
- compliance alignment  
- continual improvement opportunities  

This process supports structured executive oversight and management accountability.

---

# Review Scope

This quarterly review includes governance visibility across:

| Governance Area | Review Focus |
|---|---|
| Risk Management | Critical and emerging risks |
| Incident Management | Incident trends and escalation activities |
| Business Continuity | Resilience and recovery readiness |
| Supplier Governance | Third-party dependencies and operational exposure |
| Audit & Compliance | Findings, remediation, and evidence tracking |
| Governance KPIs | Security governance performance indicators |
| Operational Changes | Significant infrastructure or business changes |
