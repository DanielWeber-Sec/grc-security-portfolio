# Access Control Policy

---

## Purpose

To ensure that access to systems and data is granted based on business needs and follows the principle of least privilege.

---

## Scope

This policy applies to all users, systems, and applications within the ISMS scope.

---

## Policy Statements

- Access rights must be assigned based on role and business requirements
- Privileged access must be restricted and regularly reviewed
- User accounts must be uniquely identifiable
- Access must be revoked immediately upon termination or role change

---

## Responsibilities

- IT is responsible for access provisioning and deprovisioning
- Management is responsible for approving access rights
- Users are responsible for protecting their credentials

---

## Enforcement

Violations of this policy may result in disciplinary action and access revocation.

---

## Review

This policy must be reviewed annually or after significant changes.
