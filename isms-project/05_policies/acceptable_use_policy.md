# Acceptable Use Policy

---

## Purpose

To define acceptable and secure use of organizational systems, data, and resources.

This policy aims to reduce risks arising from improper or insecure use of IT systems.

---

## Scope

This policy applies to all users, including:

- employees  
- contractors  
- third-party users  

within the defined ISMS scope.

---

## Policy Statements

- Systems and data must be used for authorized business purposes only  
- Users must not engage in activities that could harm systems, networks, or data  
- Installation of unauthorized software is prohibited  
- Access credentials must not be shared  
- Users must not bypass security controls or policies  
- Suspicious activities or security incidents must be reported immediately  

---

## User Responsibilities

- protect login credentials and access rights  
- follow defined security policies and procedures  
- use systems in a responsible and secure manner  

---

## Prohibited Activities

- unauthorized access to systems or data  
- use of systems for illegal activities  
- introduction of malware or malicious software  
- misuse of company resources  

---

## Enforcement

Violations of this policy may result in:

- access restriction or removal  
- disciplinary actions  
- legal consequences where applicable  

---

## Review

This policy must be reviewed periodically or when significant changes occur in systems or security requirements.
