# Scope Definition

---

## Objective

Define the boundaries of the ISMS to ensure a clear and manageable scope.

---

## In Scope

- Development environment
- Supporting IT systems
- Cloud-based collaboration tools
- Data related to product development and operations

---

## Out of Scope

- Corporate IT outside the development domain
- Physical security of external supplier locations
- Non-critical internal systems

---

## Rationale

The selected scope focuses on areas with the highest business impact and risk exposure, particularly those affecting operational continuity and data protection.
