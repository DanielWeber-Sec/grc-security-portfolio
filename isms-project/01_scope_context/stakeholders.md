
# Stakeholders

---

## Objective

Identify key stakeholders involved in or affected by the ISMS.

---

## Internal Stakeholders

- Management (accountability and decision-making)  
- IT (implementation and operations)  
- Development teams (system usage and data handling)  

---

## External Stakeholders

- Suppliers and partners  
- Customers  
- Regulatory authorities  

---

## Responsibilities

- Management defines risk acceptance and priorities  
- IT ensures implementation of controls  
- Users follow defined policies and procedures
