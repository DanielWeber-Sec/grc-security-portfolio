# Control Mapping Overview

---

## Objective

Provide a structured mapping between identified risks, implemented controls, and relevant frameworks.

---

## Approach

The mapping connects:

- risks identified in the risk assessment  
- controls selected during risk treatment  
- ISO 27001 Annex A control references  

---

## Structure

Each mapping includes:

- risk reference  
- control reference  
- control objective  
- implementation status  

---

## Purpose

The mapping ensures:

- traceability between risks and controls  
- consistency in control implementation  
- support for audit and compliance activities  

---

## Example

| Risk | Control | Objective | Status |
|------|--------|-----------|--------|
| Ransomware | Backup & Recovery | Ensure data availability | Implemented |
| Phishing | Awareness Training | Reduce user-related risk | Planned |
| Unauthorized Access | Access Control | Protect systems and data | Implemented |

---

## Outcome

The control mapping provides a transparent link between risk management and control implementation and supports audit readiness.
