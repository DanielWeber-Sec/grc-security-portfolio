# ISMS Implementation (ISO 27001)

## Scenario

Mid-sized automotive supplier (Germany)
Business-critical environment with high dependency on production uptime and supplier integration.

## Scope

* Office IT
* Production systems
* Supplier interfaces

## Key Assets

* ERP System
* Production systems
* Customer data

## Risk Overview

| Asset      | Risk        | Impact | Likelihood |
| ---------- | ----------- | ------ | ---------- |
| ERP        | Data breach | High   | Medium     |
| Production | Downtime    | High   | High       |

## Controls

* Access Control
* Backup
* Supplier Management

## Outcome

Basic ISMS aligned with ISO 27001 principles.

## Business Impact

- Reduction of production downtime risk
- Improved protection of sensitive customer data
- Increased operational resilience

## Management Perspective

This ISMS enables structured risk-based decision-making and aligns security with overall business objectives.
