# ISMS Implementation Project (ISO 27001 Aligned)

---

## Overview

This project demonstrates a structured implementation of an Information Security Management System (ISMS) aligned with ISO 27001.

It reflects a practical, business-oriented approach to information security governance, focusing on risk-based decision-making, control implementation, and audit readiness.

The ISMS is designed not only as a compliance framework, but as a **decision-support system for management**, translating technical risks into business impact and operational consequences.

---

## Scenario

The ISMS is designed for a mid-sized automotive supplier operating in a production-driven and regulated environment.

Key characteristics include:

- high dependency on system availability
- integration with external suppliers
- handling of sensitive operational and customer data
- increasing use of cloud-based collaboration tools

This environment requires security measures that are not only effective, but also aligned with operational realities and business priorities.

---

## Structure

The repository follows the full lifecycle of an ISMS:

1. **Scope & Context**  
   Definition of scope, business environment, assets, and stakeholders

2. **Risk Assessment**  
   Identification and evaluation of risks using a structured methodology

3. **Risk Treatment**  
   Definition of mitigation strategies and control selection

4. **Statement of Applicability (SoA)**  
   Central control overview linking risks to implemented measures

5. **Security Policies**  
   Formalized policies defining security rules and responsibilities

6. **Control Mapping**  
   Mapping of controls to standards and frameworks

7. **Compliance Extension**  
   Integration of regulatory requirements (e.g., NIS2, GDPR)

8. **Audit**  
   Evaluation of ISMS effectiveness and identification of gaps

9. **Tabletop Exercise**  
   Simulated incident scenario demonstrating response capabilities

## Outcome

The result is a structured and implementable ISMS foundation that:

- reflects real-world operational constraints
- supports risk-based decision-making at management level
- ensures traceability from risk identification to control implementation
- enables continuous improvement of security posture

The focus is not only on compliance, but on **practical applicability and measurable risk reduction**.

---

## Purpose

The purpose of this project is to demonstrate a practical and structured approach to building and operating an ISMS in a realistic business scenario.

It emphasizes **clarity, traceability, and business alignment** over theoretical completeness.
