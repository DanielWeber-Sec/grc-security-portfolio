# Incident Response Policy

## Objective
Ensure effective identification, handling, and resolution of security incidents.

## Scope
- IT systems
- Production systems
- Supplier connections

## Definition

A security incident is any event that compromises confidentiality, integrity, or availability of information.

## Policy

- All security incidents must be reported immediately
- Incidents must be classified based on severity and impact
- A defined response process must be followed:
  - Identification
  - Containment
  - Eradication
  - Recovery
  - Lessons Learned
- Evidence must be preserved for analysis
- Communication must be coordinated and controlled

## Responsibilities

- Employees must report incidents
- IT/Security team handles response and investigation
- Management oversees escalation and decision-making

## Business Impact

- Reduces damage from security incidents
- Enables faster recovery
- Supports regulatory compliance
