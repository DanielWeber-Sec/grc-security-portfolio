# Backup Policy

## Objective
Ensure availability and recoverability of critical business systems and data.

## Scope
- ERP systems
- Production systems (OT)
- Customer data

## Policy

- Regular backups must be performed for all critical systems
- Backups must be protected against unauthorized access and modification
- At least one backup copy must be stored offsite or logically separated
- Backup retention periods must be defined based on business requirements
- Backup restoration must be tested regularly

## Responsibilities

- IT is responsible for backup execution and monitoring
- Management ensures compliance and provides necessary resources

## Business Impact

- Prevents data loss
- Enables recovery after ransomware or system failure
- Supports business continuity
