# Security Policies

---

## Overview

This section contains core policies supporting the ISMS.

The policies are designed to be:

- concise  
- practical  
- aligned with ISO 27001 expectations  

They define the **minimum security baseline** required to protect critical assets.

---

## Policy Structure

Each policy includes:

- Objective  
- Scope  
- Principles  
- Responsibilities  

The goal is to ensure clarity without unnecessary complexity.

---

## Included Policies

### Access Control Policy

Defines how access to systems and data is:

- granted  
- managed  
- reviewed  

Focus on least privilege and accountability.

---

### Backup & Recovery Policy

Defines requirements for:

- data backups  
- recovery procedures  
- availability assurance  

Focus on ensuring business continuity.

---

### Incident Response Policy

Defines how security incidents are:

- identified  
- reported  
- handled  

Focus on minimizing impact and restoring normal operations quickly.

---

## Approach

The policies are intentionally lightweight and realistic.

They are designed to:

- support daily operations  
- be understandable for non-security stakeholders  
- provide a foundation for further maturity  

The emphasis lies on **usability and effectiveness**, not documentation volume.
