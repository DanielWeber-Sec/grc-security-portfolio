# Lessons Learned

---

## Observations

- incident detection was delayed due to limited monitoring
- backup systems were available but not regularly tested
- roles and responsibilities were not clearly defined

---

## Improvements

- implement centralized monitoring
- introduce regular backup testing
- clarify incident response roles and escalation paths

---

## Strategic Perspective

Incidents highlight gaps not visible during normal operations.

They provide valuable input for improving resilience and strengthening the ISMS.

---

## Outcome

The tabletop exercise supports continuous improvement and ensures that security measures are not only defined but also effective in practice.
