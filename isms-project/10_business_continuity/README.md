# Business Continuity

## Objective

To ensure the organization can maintain or quickly resume critical operations in case of disruptions.

---

## Approach

- Identify critical business processes  
- Define recovery strategies  
- Establish continuity plans and responsibilities  

---

## Key Decisions

- Focus on critical processes and dependencies  
- Align recovery objectives with business priorities  
- Keep plans practical and actionable  

---

## Outcome

- Defined business continuity strategy  
- Clear recovery structures and responsibilities  
- Integration with incident response and ISMS governance
