# Risk Treatment Strategy

---

## Objective

Define how identified risks are handled in a structured and consistent manner.

---

## Treatment Options

Each risk is evaluated and assigned one of the following strategies:

- **Mitigate** → reduce likelihood or impact through controls  
- **Avoid** → eliminate the risk by removing the activity  
- **Transfer** → shift risk (e.g., insurance, outsourcing)  
- **Accept** → tolerate the risk within defined thresholds  

---

## Decision Criteria

The selected strategy is based on:

- risk level (score)  
- business impact  
- cost and feasibility of controls  
- regulatory and compliance requirements  

---

## Principles

- High and critical risks must be treated  
- Medium risks are evaluated and treated if necessary  
- Low risks may be accepted with monitoring  

---

## Outcome

The strategy ensures consistent, business-aligned decisions on how risks are addressed within the ISMS.
