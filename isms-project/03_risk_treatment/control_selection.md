# Control Selection

---

## Objective

Define how appropriate security controls are selected based on identified risks.

---

## Approach

Controls are selected using a risk-based approach:

- identify risk drivers  
- determine required control objectives  
- map to ISO 27001 Annex A controls  

---

## Selection Criteria

- effectiveness in reducing risk  
- relevance to business processes  
- alignment with regulatory requirements  
- feasibility of implementation  

---

## Examples

- Access Control → mitigate unauthorized access risks  
- Backup → reduce impact of ransomware  
- Logging & Monitoring → improve detection capabilities  

---

## Outcome

Selected controls directly address identified risks and form the basis for the Statement of Applicability (SoA).
