# Residual Risk

---

## Objective

Assess and document the remaining risk after implementing treatment measures.

---

## Definition

Residual risk is the level of risk that remains after controls have been applied.

---

## Approach

- reassess likelihood and impact after control implementation  
- calculate updated risk score  
- determine acceptability  

---

## Acceptance Criteria

Residual risks may be accepted if:

- they fall within defined risk tolerance  
- further mitigation is not cost-effective  
- business justification exists  

---

## Governance

- residual risks must be approved by management  
- acceptance decisions must be documented  

---

## Outcome

Residual risk evaluation ensures transparency and supports informed decision-making at the management level.
