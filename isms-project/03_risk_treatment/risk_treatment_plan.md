# Risk Treatment Plan

---

## Objective

Define concrete actions to address identified risks and track their implementation.

---

## Structure

Each risk treatment includes:

- risk reference  
- selected treatment strategy  
- defined control or measure  
- responsible owner  
- implementation status  

---

## Example

| Risk | Treatment | Control | Owner | Status |
|------|----------|--------|-------|--------|
| Ransomware | Mitigate | Backup & Recovery | IT | Implemented |
| Phishing | Mitigate | Awareness Training | HR / IT | Planned |
| Supplier Risk | Mitigate | Supplier Assessment | Procurement | In Progress |

---

## Approach

Treatment measures are prioritized based on:

- risk severity  
- business impact  
- implementation feasibility  

---

## Outcome

The risk treatment plan ensures that mitigation measures are clearly defined, assigned, and tracked.
