# 07 – Compliance Extension

---

## Overview

This section extends the ISMS beyond ISO 27001 by incorporating relevant regulatory and industry frameworks.

It demonstrates how the ISMS can be aligned with additional requirements such as NIS2 and GDPR.

---

## Purpose

The objective is to:

- bridge the gap between ISO 27001 and regulatory requirements
- ensure broader compliance coverage
- support organizations operating in regulated environments

---

## Approach

The ISMS is extended by:

- mapping controls to regulatory requirements
- identifying overlaps and gaps
- aligning security measures with legal obligations

---

## Included Frameworks

- NIS2 Directive
- General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)

---

## Outcome

The result is a more comprehensive and realistic ISMS that:

- aligns with regulatory expectations
- supports audit and compliance activities
- reflects real-world security requirements

---

## Note

This section provides a simplified but practical view of how ISO-based ISMS structures can be extended to meet regulatory demands.
