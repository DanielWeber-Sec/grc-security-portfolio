# Framework Comparison

---

## Objective

Provide a high-level comparison of ISO 27001, NIS2, and GDPR.

---

## Comparison

| Area | ISO 27001 | NIS2 | GDPR |
|------|----------|------|------|
| Focus | Security management system | Regulatory cybersecurity | Data protection |
| Approach | Risk-based | Regulatory obligations | Legal requirements |
| Scope | Organization-wide | Critical entities | Personal data |
| Enforcement | Certification | Regulatory authority | Legal enforcement |
| Key Strength | Structured framework | Mandatory compliance | Data protection focus |

---

## Interpretation

- ISO 27001 provides the structural foundation  
- NIS2 adds regulatory enforcement and obligations  
- GDPR focuses on protection of personal data  

---

## Conclusion

Together, these frameworks complement each other and form a comprehensive approach to security and compliance.
