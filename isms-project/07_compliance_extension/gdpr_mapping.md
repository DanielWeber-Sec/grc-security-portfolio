# GDPR Alignment

---

## Objective

Show how the ISMS supports GDPR requirements related to data protection and security.

---

## Approach

GDPR is supported through security and governance measures implemented within the ISMS.

---

## Key Areas & ISMS Support

| GDPR Area               | ISMS Contribution                         |
| ----------------------- | ----------------------------------------- |
| Data Security (Art. 32) | Access control, encryption, backup        |
| Risk Management         | Risk assessment and treatment processes   |
| Incident Handling       | Incident Response Policy                  |
| Access Control          | User access management and authentication |
| Logging & Monitoring    | Detection of unauthorized access          |
| Supplier Management     | Third-party risk controls                 |

---

## Practical Perspective

The ISMS provides a structured approach to implementing technical and organizational measures (TOMs).

It ensures that data protection is embedded into security processes rather than treated separately.

---

## Limitations

GDPR includes legal and organizational requirements beyond security, which must be addressed separately.

---

## Conclusion

A well-implemented ISMS significantly contributes to GDPR compliance by ensuring structured and risk-based security measures.
