# Risk Assessment – Automotive Supplier

## Objective

Identify and evaluate key risks in a production-focused company.

## Methodology

Impact vs Likelihood approach.

## Risk Register

| Risk          | Impact | Likelihood | Mitigation            |
| ------------- | ------ | ---------- | --------------------- |
| Ransomware    | High   | Medium     | Backup & segmentation |
| Phishing      | Medium | High       | Awareness training    |
| Supplier Risk | High   | Low        | Vendor checks         |

## Business Impact

* Financial loss due to downtime
* Reputational damage from data breaches
* Operational disruption

## Priority Risks

1. Production downtime (critical)
2. Phishing attacks (high likelihood)
3. Supplier vulnerabilities (strategic risk)
