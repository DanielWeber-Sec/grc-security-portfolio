# Audit Checklist

---

## Risk Management

- Is a formal risk assessment process defined?
- Are risks documented and regularly reviewed?
- Is risk treatment clearly defined and tracked?

---

## Access Control

- Are user access rights defined and documented?
- Is access reviewed periodically?
- Are privileged accounts controlled?

---

## Backup & Recovery

- Are backups performed regularly?
- Are backups tested?
- Is recovery time defined and achievable?

---

## Incident Management

- Is an incident response process defined?
- Are incidents documented and analyzed?
- Are lessons learned integrated?

---

## Monitoring

- Is logging implemented?
- Are logs reviewed regularly?
- Is suspicious activity detected?

---

## Supplier Management

- Are suppliers assessed for security risks?
- Are requirements defined contractually?
