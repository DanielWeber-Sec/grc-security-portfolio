# Audit

---

## Overview

This module outlines how the ISMS can be reviewed and evaluated through structured audit activities.

---

## Objective

To assess:

- effectiveness of implemented controls
- compliance with defined policies
- alignment with ISO 27001 principles

---

## Scope

The audit focuses on key ISMS components:

- risk management
- control implementation
- policy framework
- monitoring and incident handling

---

## Components

- audit approach
- audit checklist
- example findings

---

## Outcome

Provides transparency on ISMS effectiveness and identifies areas for improvement.
