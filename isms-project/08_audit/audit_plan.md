# Audit Approach

---

## Objective

Define a structured approach to evaluating the ISMS.

---

## Method

The audit is conducted using:

- document review  
- stakeholder interviews  
- control verification  

---

## Focus Areas

- risk assessment and treatment  
- control implementation  
- policy adherence  
- incident handling processes  

---

## Principles

- risk-based auditing  
- evidence-driven evaluation  
- focus on effectiveness, not only existence  

---

## Output

The audit results in:

- identified gaps  
- improvement recommendations  
- prioritization of findings  

---

## Perspective

The audit is not only a compliance activity, but a tool to improve security posture and operational resilience.
