# Tabletop Exercise – Ransomware Scenario

---

## Overview

This exercise simulates a ransomware incident in a production-driven environment.

It is designed to validate incident response capabilities and business continuity measures.

---

## Objectives

- test detection and escalation processes  
- evaluate containment and recovery procedures  
- assess decision-making under pressure  
- validate communication between stakeholders  

---

## Scenario

A ransomware attack impacts systems critical to production and data processing.

The incident requires coordinated response across IT, security, and business units.

---

## Outcome

The exercise provides insights into:

- gaps in response processes  
- effectiveness of defined policies  
- organizational readiness  

It serves as a validation layer between documented procedures and real-world execution.
