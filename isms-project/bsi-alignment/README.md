# BSI IT-Grundschutz Alignment

---

## Objective

To align the ISMS structure with German BSI IT-Grundschutz principles and demonstrate compatibility with national security standards.

---

## Approach

The existing ISO 27001-based ISMS is mapped to:

- BSI IT-Grundschutz methodology
- baseline security requirements
- structured protection needs assessment

---

## Key Alignment Areas

### 1. Asset Structure

- Identification of systems and processes
- Mapping to IT-Grundschutz components

---

### 2. Protection Needs Assessment

Assets are evaluated based on:

- confidentiality
- integrity
- availability

---

### 3. Typical Threats

Consideration of:

- malware and ransomware
- unauthorized access
- system failures

---

### 4. Security Measures

Alignment of controls with:

- BSI baseline requirements
- organizational and technical safeguards

---

## Value

This alignment demonstrates that the ISMS:

- can be adapted to German regulatory expectations
- supports structured and standardized security approaches
- is suitable for organizations operating in regulated environments

---

## Conclusion

The ISMS is compatible with both ISO 27001 and BSI IT-Grundschutz principles, enabling flexible adoption depending on regulatory requirements.
