# Supplier Security Questionnaire

## Objective

This document demonstrates a governance-oriented supplier security questionnaire used to assess the security maturity, operational resilience, and governance capabilities of third-party providers.

The objective is to support:

- supplier due diligence
- operational resilience
- governance transparency
- risk-informed onboarding
- regulatory alignment
- audit readiness

The questionnaire helps organizations gather structured information about supplier security and governance practices before or during supplier relationships.

---

## Governance Perspective

Supplier security questionnaires are part of structured Third-Party Risk Management (TPRM).

The purpose is not only to verify technical controls, but also to evaluate whether suppliers maintain appropriate governance, resilience, and operational security practices aligned with organizational expectations.

The depth of assessment should scale according to supplier classification and operational criticality.

## Executive Interpretation

Organizations should not rely solely on contractual agreements when evaluating supplier security maturity.

Structured governance-oriented questionnaires improve transparency into supplier governance practices, resilience capabilities, and operational risk exposure.

This supports more informed onboarding decisions and strengthens long-term operational resilience.

Supplier security questionnaires are a practical governance mechanism used to assess supplier maturity, identify governance gaps, and support risk-informed supplier oversight.
